About Us

Mountain Movers CBO is a community-based organization founded to support and empower families through collective savings, emergency preparedness, and welfare initiatives. Our mission is to build a resilient and united community.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

A thriving Kawangware community where youth, girls, and women lead empowered, self-sufficient lives.

Mission

To equip youth, girls, and women with the skills, mentorship, and resources needed to break barriers, unlock potential, and inspire community-driven transformation.
