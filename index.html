About Us

Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware by supporting and uplifting youth, girls, and women through empowerment programs, sports and mentorship.

Our Services

  • Youth Empowerment: Life skills training, Sports & Talent nurturing
  • Women Empowerment: Economic empowerment, Health and wellness awareness
  • Girls' Support Programs: Menstrual hygiene education, Sanitary towel distribution, Self-esteem & mentorship sessions
