<section id="about">

<h2 class="section-title">About Us</h2>

<p>Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware by supporting and uplifting **youth, girls, and women** through comprehensive empowerment programs, sports, and mentorship.</p>

<h3>Our Vision</h3>

<p>A self-reliant community where youth, women, and girls thrive in dignity and empowerment.</p>

<h3>Our Mission</h3>

<p>To empower the vulnerable in Kawangware through life skills, mentorship, education support, and economic opportunities that foster sustainable development and social inclusion.</p>

</section>


<section id="programs">

<h2 class="section-title">Our Programs</h2>

<div class="programs">

<div class="card">

<h3>Youth Empowerment</h3>

<p>We provide life skills training and create platforms for young people to discover, nurture, and showcase their talents through sports and creative arts. Our mentorship sessions help them build confidence, discipline, and purpose, guiding them towards a brighter future.</p>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h3>Women Empowerment</h3>

<p>We equip women with vital economic skills through vocational training and entrepreneurship development, fostering financial independence. Our sessions on health and wellness empower them to lead healthier, informed lives, significantly boosting their capacity to support their families and communities.</p>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h3>Girls’ Support Programs</h3>

<p>We champion girls' well-being by promoting essential menstrual hygiene education, ensuring access to sanitary towels, and offering dedicated mentorship to boost their self-esteem and encourage leadership. We also help those from vulnerable families stay in school by contributing towards their school fees, breaking barriers to education.</p>

</div>

</div>

</section>
