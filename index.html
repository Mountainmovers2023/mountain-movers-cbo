About Us

Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware by supporting and uplifting youth, girls, and women through empowerment programs, sports and mentorship.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

A thriving Kawangware community where youth, girls, and women lead empowered, self-sufficient lives.

Mission

To equip youth, girls, and women with the skills, mentorship, and resources needed to break barriers, unlock potential, and inspire community-driven transformation.

Our Programs

Youth Empowerment

We support young people through life skills training, sports & talent development, and mentorship. Our goal is to foster innovation, leadership, and self-reliance among the youth.

Women Empowerment

Through capacity building, economic empowerment initiatives, and health awareness, we help women build sustainable livelihoods and promote their overall well-being.

We are committed to empowering girls through targeted programs that address their immediate and long-term needs.
