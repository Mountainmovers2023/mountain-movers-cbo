About Us

Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware by supporting and uplifting youth, girls, and women through empowerment programs, sports, and mentorship.

Our Vision

A self-reliant community where youth, women, and girls thrive in dignity and empowerment.

Our Mission

To empower the vulnerable in Kawangware through life skills, mentorship, education support, and economic opportunities that foster sustainable development and social inclusion.

Our Programs

Youth Empowerment

Training youth in life skills, mentorship, and leadership. Providing sports and creative outlets for their growth.

Women Empowerment

Equipping women with vocational skills and supporting financial independence through entrepreneurship training.

Girls’ Support

Providing menstrual hygiene support, school fees assistance, and confidence-building mentorship sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mountain Movers CBO?

We are a community-based organization in Kawangware, Nairobi, empowering youth, girls, and women through mentorship, education, sports, and life skills programs.

Where is your organization based?

We are based in Kawangware, a vibrant but underserved community in Nairobi, Kenya. Our programs are centered around local schools and community grounds.

How can I donate?

1. Via M-PESA (Kenya): Use Paybill 522533 and Account 8065266.

2. From Abroad: Use apps like Remitly or WorldRemit to the Paybill above, or Bank Wire to KCB Bank using SWIFT: KCBKKE NA.

3. In-Kind: We accept books and sports gear. Message us to coordinate.

How will my donation be used?

Donations support school supplies, menstrual kits (Dignity Packs), vocational training for women, and sports equipment for our girls' soccer team. Every coin goes directly to impacting lives in Kawangware.
