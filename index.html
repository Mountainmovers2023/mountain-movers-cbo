About Us

Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware by supporting and uplifting youth, girls, and women through empowerment programs, sports and mentorship.

Our Services

Youth Empowerment

  • Life skills training
  • Sports & talent nurturing

Women Empowerment

  • Economic empowerment (skills training & entrepreneurship)
  • Health and wellness awareness

Girls' Support Programs

  • Menstrual hygiene education
  • Sanitary towel distribution
  • Self-esteem & mentorship sessions
