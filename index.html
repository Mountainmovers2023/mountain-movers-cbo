About Us

Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware by supporting and uplifting youth, girls, and women through empowerment programs, sports and mentorship.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

A thriving Kawangware community where youth, girls, and women lead empowered, self-sufficient lives.

Mission

To equip youth, girls, and women with the skills, mentorship, and resources needed to break barriers, unlock potential, and inspire community-driven transformation.

Our Programs

Youth Empowerment

  • Life skills training
  • Sports & talent nurturing

Women Empowerment

  • Skills training & entrepreneurship
  • Health and wellness awareness

Girls' Support Programs

  • Menstrual hygiene education
  • Sanitary towel support
  • Self-esteem & mentorship
