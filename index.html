About Our Mission

Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware. We support and uplift youth, girls, and women through targeted empowerment programs, sports, and life-changing mentorship.

Our Vision

A self-reliant community where youth, women, and girls thrive in dignity, empowered to overcome socio-economic challenges and lead meaningful lives.

Our Mission

To empower the vulnerable in Kawangware through life skills, mentorship, education, and economic opportunities, fostering a culture of resilience and leadership.

Our Impact Areas

Three pillars of change for a stronger Kawangware.

🏆

Youth Empowerment

We train youth in life skills, leadership, and teamwork. Through sports and creative outlets, we provide a safe space for growth and mentorship.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

We equip women with vocational skills, financial literacy, and entrepreneurial training to foster sustainable independence and dignity.

🎒

Girls’ Support

Providing dignity packs (hygiene kits), school supplies, and confidence-building workshops to ensure no girl stays behind.

Real Stories

Lives transformed through your generosity and our programs.

Celine's Journey: EDUCATION

"Before Mountain Movers, I lived in fear of my monthly cycle. I would miss at least one week of school every month because I couldn't afford sanitary towels. Today, I am the captain of our girls' soccer team and I haven't missed a day of class this year. I am proof that when you empower a girl, you empower a nation."

RESULT: 100% School Attendance & Team Captain

YOUTH LEADERSHIP: Faith Leading the Way

"Growing up in Kawangware, the streets can pull you in the wrong direction quickly. The Mountain Movers Mentorship program gave me a reason to stay disciplined and focused. I’ve gone from being a girl on the sidelines to a mentor for younger children in our community soccer league."

RESULT: Certified Community Peer Mentor

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mountain Movers CBO?

We are a community-based organization in Kawangware, Nairobi, empowering youth, girls, and women through mentorship, education, sports, and life skills programs.

Where is your organization based?

We are based in Kawangware, a vibrant but underserved community in Nairobi, Kenya. Our programs are centered around local schools and community grounds.

How can I donate?

1. Via M-PESA (Kenya): Use Paybill 522533 and Account 8065266.

2. From Abroad: Use apps like Remitly or WorldRemit to the Paybill above, or Bank Wire to KCB Bank using SWIFT: KCBKKE NA.

3. In-Kind: We accept books and sports gear. Message us to coordinate.

How will my donation be used?

Donations support school supplies, menstrual kits (Dignity Packs), vocational training for women, and sports equipment for our girls' soccer team. Every coin goes directly to impacting lives in Kawangware.
