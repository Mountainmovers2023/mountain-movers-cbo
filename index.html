About Us

Mountain Movers Community-Based Organization (CBO) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming lives in Kawangware by supporting and uplifting youth, girls, and women through empowerment programs, sports, and mentorship.

Our Vision

A self-reliant community where youth, women, and girls thrive in dignity and empowerment.

Our Mission

To empower the vulnerable in Kawangware through life skills, mentorship, education, and economic opportunities.

Our Impact Areas

🏆

Youth Empowerment

Training youth in life skills, leadership, and teamwork through sports and creative mentorship outlets.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

Equipping women with vocational skills and financial literacy to foster sustainable independence.

🎒

Girls’ Support

Providing dignity packs (hygiene kits), school supplies, and confidence-building workshops.

Real Impact, Real Stories

Your support isn't just a number on a spreadsheet; it’s the reason these leaders in Kawangware can dream of a brighter future.

Impact: Education

Celine's Journey

"Before Mountain Movers, I lived in fear of my monthly cycle. I would miss at least one week of school every month because we couldn't afford sanitary towels. I fell behind in my grades and lost my confidence."

"Today, everything has changed. Through the Dignity Pack program, I have the supplies I need to stay in class. I am no longer just a student; I am the captain of our girls' soccer team. I am proof that when you empower a girl, you empower a nation."

RESULT: 100% School Attendance
Impact: Youth Leadership

Leading the Way

"Growing up in Kawangware, the streets can easily pull you in the wrong direction. I spent most of my days idle, with no vision for my future and no one to guide me toward my potential."

"The Mountain Movers Mentorship program gave me a reason to stay disciplined. Soccer was the hook, but the leadership workshops were the foundation. I’ve gone from being a girl on the sidelines to a mentor for younger children in my neighborhood."

RESULT: Community Peer Mentor

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mountain Movers CBO?

We are a community-based organization in Kawangware, Nairobi, empowering youth, girls, and women through mentorship, education, sports, and life skills programs.

Where is your organization based?

We are based in Kawangware, a vibrant but underserved community in Nairobi, Kenya. Our programs are centered around local schools and community grounds.

How can I donate?

1. Via M-PESA (Kenya): Use Paybill 522533 and Account 8065266.

2. From Abroad: Use apps like Remitly or WorldRemit to the Paybill above, or Bank Wire to KCB Bank using SWIFT: KCBKKE NA.

3. In-Kind: We accept books and sports gear. Message us to coordinate.

How will my donation be used?

Donations support school supplies, menstrual kits (Dignity Packs), vocational training for women, and sports equipment for our girls' soccer team. Every coin goes directly to impacting lives in Kawangware.
